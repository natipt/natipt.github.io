Natalia María Pacheco-Tallaj

I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics at MIT. Before that, I was an undergraduate at Harvard until 2020 and a Fulbright Fellow at the University of Copenhagen until 2021.

I'm interested in the interplay between algebraic topology, (derived) algebraic geometry, and problems in quantum matter and supersymmetric field theory. I am also interested in applied geometry, specifically to problems in computer graphics and physics simulation. I was a Research Scientist Intern at the Adobe 3D Graphics and Interactive Design Lab in the summers 2024 and 2025.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, unnecessarily elaborate spreadsheets, and cooking vegetarian meals. I am from San Juan, Puerto Rico.
